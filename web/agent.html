The decision policy

retry thresholdrisk ≥ 0.35 → retry on the same provider
reroute thresholdrisk ≥ 0.55 → switch to a healthy backup
reversibilityevery action is reversible and fully logged
latency trade-off+0.110s avg latency for 52.07% fewer failures

LEO acts on forecasted failure risk rather than observed errors. Below the low-risk threshold it stays on the primary provider; between 0.35 and 0.55 it retries; above 0.55 it reroutes ahead of the failure. The modest latency cost — about 110 ms per call — buys a halving of the failure rate, and because every decision is reversible and logged, the policy is safe to run in production and trivial to audit.
