A bidirectional memory network with attention pooling

Two stacked bidirectional LSTM layers consume a 30-step window of 43 input signals. A learned attention pooler decides which timesteps mattered, and three sigmoid heads emit calibrated failure probabilities at h + 1, h + 5 and h + 15 minutes — simultaneously.

Scheduler

scaler fit on first 80% only — no leakage

30 epochs
