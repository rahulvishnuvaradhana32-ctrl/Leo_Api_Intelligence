Architecture

A bidirectional memory network with attention pooling

A 30-step window of 43 telemetry signals flows through a convolutional feature extractor, a stacked bi-directional LSTM and a multi-head attention pooler, then out to three calibrated heads that forecast failure at h + 1, h + 5 and h + 15 minutes — in a single forward pass.

The forward path

raw telemetry → calibrated, multi-horizon forecast · one pass

1 · Telemetry ingest

43 engineered signals — error rates, latency, throughput and volatility — streamed as 30-step sequences per API.

2 · Conv1d extractor

A 1-D convolution sweeps the window for local precursor patterns before the recurrent stack sees them.

3 · Bi-LSTM

Two stacked bidirectional LSTM layers (hidden 256 · 2 layers) read the sequence forward and backward for full temporal context.

4 · Multi-head attention

Learned attention heads score each timestep and pool them into a single weighted summary — the timesteps that mattered most.

5 · Three horizon heads

Three sigmoid heads emit independent failure probabilities at 1 / 5 / 15 min simultaneously.

6 · Calibration

Temperature scaling tempers each score, then inductive conformal prediction wraps it with a 90% coverage guarantee.
