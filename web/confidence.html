What the band means operationally

Each forecast ships with an interval whose miscoverage is capped at 10% — a calibrated, defensible error rate rather than an uncalibrated probability. h+1 and h+5 hold coverage with budget to spare; h+15 lands at 89.98%, 0.02% under target and well inside Monte-Carlo noise, so we treat the longest horizon as advisory: the next recalibration widens q̂ to clear margin. The guarantee is exchangeability-based and distribution-free, so it survives model retrains and shifting traffic mix without re-deriving assumptions.
